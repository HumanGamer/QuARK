<b>Add Camera Position</b>: This item only appears on the
<a href="intro.mapeditor.rmb_menus.noselectionmenu.html">No Selection RMB menu</a> when the <g>RMB</g>
is clicked in the 3D view and nothing is selected. When selected, this will open
<a href="intro.mapeditor.rmb_menus.noselectionmenu.html#addcamerapos">its dialog box</a>
(shown to the right here) where you can add a new 'Camera view' for a quick jump to see your work from
a different preset angle when this camera icon is clicked in the
<a href="intro.mapeditor.dataforms.html#treeview">Tree-view</a>.
This is the final extended item of this menu. Detailed information on its use and capabilities
can be found in its own section under
<a href="intro.mapeditor.floating3dview.html#camera">Floating 3D windows</a> in the Infobase docs.
