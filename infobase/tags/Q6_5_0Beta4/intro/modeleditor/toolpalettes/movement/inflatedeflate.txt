title: Shift Left/Right selection
author: cdunde

<img border=1 align=right>inflatedeflate.png</img>

Shift the selected vertexes or faces (not dependable for faces) Left or Right by an
amount specified in the <ref> ./configuration </ref> (last button of this toolbar).

Shifting Left or Right means moving the selected items by a fixed amount of pixels,
negative values shifts the selection to the left, positive values shifts the selection to the right.
