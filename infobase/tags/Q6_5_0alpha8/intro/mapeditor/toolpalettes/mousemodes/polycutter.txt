title: Polyhedron cutter
author: Decker

<img border=0 align=right>polycutter.png</img>
<i>"Like a knife through butter."</i>

Mark this icon in the mouse-modes toolbox, when you want to cut polyhedrons in
two by dragging a line through the polyhedrons.

Remember you can always undo your actions if you're not satisfied with the
result.
