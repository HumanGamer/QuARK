Icon Objects are the individual items of Image Lists.

<table border=1 cellspacing=0 cellpadding=4>

<tr><td class="doccode">
icon.size
</td><td>
The (x,y) size of the icon. This is the same for all icons of an image list.
</td></tr>

<tr><td class="doccode">
icon.disabledimage
</td><td>
