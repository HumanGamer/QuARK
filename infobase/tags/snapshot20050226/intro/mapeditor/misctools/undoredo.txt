The icon will open up the undo/redo display.

<i>Redo</i> Click on the entry, from where you want to redo.
Notice you can't do a selective redo-selection. Its all or nothing.

<i>Undo</i> Click on the entry, from where you want to undo.
Notice you can't do a selective undo-selection. Its all or nothing.
