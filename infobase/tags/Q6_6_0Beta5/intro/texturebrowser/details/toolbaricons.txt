This toolbar appears when viewing texture details.

The first button <img border=0 align=absmiddle>toolbaricon1.png</img> calls the
external editor
(configured in <ref> intro/configuration/general/externaleditors</ref>) to edit
the image.

The second button <img border=0 align=absmiddle>toolbaricon2.png</img> opens a
window showing you the colour palette for Quake II (or whichever game you are
editing levels for).

The third button <img border=0 align=absmiddle>toolbaricon3.png</img> allows you
to resize a texture, but only if the texture has been saved inside an add-on.
You cannot resize a texture you are linking to.
