title: Mirror selection (Symmetry)
author: cdunde

<img border=1 align=right>mirror.png</img>
These buttons will mirror your selection, around its common center. To see the
common center of your selected object(s), you must be able to see the
<ref> ../display/linear </ref> of the selection.

The icons from left to right:
<ul>
<li><i>X-symmetry</i>. Mirror around the selection's common X-axis.
<li><i>Y-symmetry</i>. Mirror around the selection's common Y-axis.
<li><i>Z-symmetry</i>. Mirror around the selection's common Z-axis.
</ul>
