Icons from left to right:

<i>New 3D-window</i> will create a new floating 3D-window, which you can place
anywhere on your desktop. It only exist as long as you are in the map editor.

<i>Full 3D-view</i> will take you to a full-screen 3D-display. You must press
Escape to return to the map editor.

<i>OpenGL 3D-view</i> will create a new floating OpenGL 3D-window, where you
will be able to preview your light effects.
