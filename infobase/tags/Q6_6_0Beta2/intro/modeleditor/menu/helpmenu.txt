<i>Contextual help</i>. Opens your web-browser.

<i>Help contents</i>. Opens your web-browser.

<i>QurK's Layout</i>. Opens your web-browser to the basic editor layout page of these docs.

<i>FAQ</i>. Opens your web-browser to the FAQ page of these docs.

<i>QuArK's web site</i>. Opens your web-browser to the on-line Official QuArK web site.

<i>QuArK's Forums site</i>. Opens your web-browser to the on-line QuArK Forums site.

<i>View console</i>. Shows a Python-console. Usefull when debugging plug-ins.

<i>About</i>. Shows the about box.
