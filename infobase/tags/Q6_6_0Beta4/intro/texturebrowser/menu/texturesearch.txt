title: Texture Search
author: cdunde

<img border=1 align=right>texturesearch.png</img>
Clicking on this icon button will bring up the <g>Search for Texture</g> dialog window.
Enter a word in the <g>Search for:</g> box that might be in the name of the texture
or type of texture you are looking for and click on the check mark button to do the search.

The dialog window will close and if any textures where found they will now be listed in the
<g>Searched Textures</g> folder.
