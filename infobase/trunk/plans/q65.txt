further down on the list something appears, the more likely it is
to be delayed till 6.6, or never to happen at all.

<LI>Game support of as many as possible games (mostly cdunde)
<br>status: never done!

<LI>OpenGL in all 3D views (DanielPharos)
<br>status: done!

<LI>Get the model editor back on track (cdunde)
<br>status: back on track!

<LI>Fully working Direct3D renderer (DanielPharos)
<br>status: only made stable basis

<LI>OpenGL shader-rendering (DanielPharos)

<LI>Left-overs from 6.4
