title: Registering at SourceForge
author: Armin Rigo, Peter Brett

Connect to the <a target="_blank" href="http://sourceforge.net">SourceForge
website</a> and register yourself as a new user. Then contact a QuArK
project administrator and give your login name so that we can add you
to the QuArK project members list. They are listed on the <link project>SourceForge website</link>
after the "Brought to you by"-text near the top of the page.
