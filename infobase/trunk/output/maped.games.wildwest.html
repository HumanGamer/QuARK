 2.6.17. Return to Castle Wolfenstein: The WildWest

  [ Prev - Up - Next ] 

"Set in the later half of the 19th century around the Western states of America bordering Mexico. A land plagued with disputes, lawlessness, gunfights and some of that periods most gruesome characters. An ex-Mexican army captain turned bandit, driven by a personal vendetta, has formed a mob of some of the most ruthless Mexican bandits to sweep a reign of terror through the land."

The WildWest is a Multiplayer modification of Return to Castle Wolfenstein (which is in turn based upon Quake 3 Arena) developed by two British gents by the name of Hewster and BoltyBoy. Please see the home of the WildWest or the Gaming Forums' WildWest Mod forum for more details.

If you have any questions, problems or find solutions regarding this topic, please post them in the QuArK forum/mailing-list.

Please note: This version of the WildWest is intended for use with the 1.5 Beta version and newer. There are Entities that are referenced in the menu structure that may not exist in earlier versions.
