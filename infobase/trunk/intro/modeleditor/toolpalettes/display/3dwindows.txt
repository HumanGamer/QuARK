<i>Full 3D view</i> will create a new floating 3D-window, which you can place
anywhere on your desktop and resize as you wish.

Additional 3D windows can be opened if the 'Allow multiple 3D windows' option is
selected in the Configuration, General, 3D view, Additional settings section.
