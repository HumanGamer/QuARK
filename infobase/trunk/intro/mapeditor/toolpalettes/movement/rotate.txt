title: Rotate selection
author: Decker

<img border=0 align=right>rotate.png</img>
These buttons will rotate your selection, the amount of degrees specified in
the <ref> ./configuration </ref>, around its common center. To see the common
center of your selected object(s), you must be able to see the linear handles
of the selection.

The icons from left to right:
<ul>
<li><i>Clockwise Z-axis rotation</i>. Rotate clockwise around the selection's common Z-axis.
<li><i>Anticlockwise Z-axis rotation</i>. Rotate anticlockwise around the selection's common Z-axis.
<br><br>
<li><i>Clockwise X-axis rotation</i>. Rotate clockwise around the selection's common X-axis.
<li><i>Anticlockwise X-axis rotation</i>. Rotate anticlockwise around the selection's common X-axis.
<br><br>
<li><i>Clockwise Y-axis rotation</i>. Rotate clockwise around the selection's common Y-axis.
<li><i>Anticlockwise Y-axis rotation</i>. Rotate anticlockwise around the selection's common Y-axis.
</ul>
